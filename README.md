# interwallet.xyz

InterWallet is a natively cross-chain cryptocurrency wallet.

- For end users, it represents a large step toward chain abstraction and simplified interaction with blockchain based applications.
- For L2s, it represnts a better overall experience for your local userbase, and better exposure to external users for your local applications.
- For the ecosystem, it represnets a shift away from economic fragmentation.

It is the *production grade* continuation of the proof of concept built during EthGlobal's EthOnline hackathon: [SCBridgeAccount](https://ethglobal.com/showcase/scbridgeaccount-ivyas), winner of top L2 sponsor prizes:

- Best on Scroll (from Scroll)
- Best Public good with Account Abstraction (from Polygon)
